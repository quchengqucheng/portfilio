Installation Design Project

Introduction

This is an installation interaction project on the theme of Chinese Opera. In this project, get immersive experience by experiencing the Chinese opera performance themselves, in order to evoke their interests and by the way to learn more about Chinese opera.

Research

Culture Research

Nowadays, people are grew up under the influence of mainstream culture and pay less attention to Chinese traditional arts. However, the quintessence of Chinese culture should be heritaged. And the contemporary people should contribute to it.

History

Traditional Chinese opera, or Xiqu, is a popular form of drama and musical theatre in China with roots going back to the early periods in China. It is a composite performance art that is an amalgamation of various art forms that existed in ancient China, and evolved gradually over more than a thousand years, reaching its mature form in the 13th century during the Song Dynasty.

Development

Early forms of Chinese theater are simple, but over time they incorporated various art forms, such as music, song and dance, martial arts, acrobatics, as well as literary art forms to become traditional Chinese opera. There are numerous regional branches of traditional Chinese opera, including the Beijing opera, Sichuan opera, Cantonese opera and etc.

Present

In the 21st century, Chinese opera is seldom publicly staged except in formal Chinese opera houses..
