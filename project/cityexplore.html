City Explore

Background

I like city exploration very much. This process is very interesting, because we can always find some strange and interesting places. They hide the beauty that I usually didn't notice and discover, which is different from the city of Beijing that I remember. I also like to use photos to document the process and the results of the exploration. Every time I share photos in a circle of friends or ins, there are always friends who wishes to go with me next time or hope I will tell them the exact location of the filming and the restrictions on the filming. So I think it's good to have a platform for providing information on urban exploration and getting people who like city exploration together.
