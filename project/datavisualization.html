Dream of the Red Chamber

Data Visualization Project

Background

The Dream of the Red Chamber describes the Jias' wealth and influence in great naturalistic detail, and charts the Jias' fall from the height of their prestige, following some thirty main characters and over four hundred minor ones. So we can see that the relationships between characters are quite complex. And I'm trying to figure out who are the main characters and what are the relationship between the them. I aim at design and coding an interactive visualization of the Dream of the Red Chamber.

Research

I go through the Dream of the Red Chamber, and mark the information that I need and note it in my notebook. And I gathered some information on the Internet to ensure the accuracy of my research.

Gathering the data

First I found the full set of Dream of the Red Chamber online. Then I coding a Web crawler by node.js and gathering the raw data of the novel.
